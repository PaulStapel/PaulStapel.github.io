I really enjoy this bbq-sauce with pulled pork or as a glaze for spare-ribs.

### Ingredients
* Ketchup
* Worcester Sauce
* Mustard
* Vinegar
* Garlic powder
* Onion powder
* Salt, pepper and sugar to taste

## Cooking plan
* Put it all into a pan and stir over low heat. Go easy on the mustard and vinegar. 

## ENJOY!
