This recipe is easy, fresh, filled with protein and takes zero effort at all. Perfect for a weekday dinner. 

## Ingredients
- Chicken thighs
- Tortilla wraps
- Avocado (optional)
- Cheddar cheese (optional)

### For the seasoning
- Olive oil
- Paprika powder
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder
- Black pepper
- Salt

### For the sauce
- Yoghurt
- Sour cream 
- Cumin powder
- Garlic powder
- Paprika powder
- Chili powder (optional)
- Mayonaisse (optional)

## Cooking plan
- Mix your chicken thighs and seasoning in a bowl and rub untill thoroughly seasoned. 
- Put your chicken thighs in the oven on 220 Celcius for about 25 - 30 minutes. 
- Meanwhile, start assembling your sauce by mixing 1 part yoghurt with 1 part sour cream and seasoning to taste with the listed spices. You may also add some mayonaisse to give it extra creaminess and fattiness. 
- Once your chicken is finished cooking, assemble your tortilla by adding the sauce, chicken (cut into strips or shredded), and optional avocado and cheddar cheese. 
- For extra crunch, grill your tortilla in a pan on high heat for a bit. then flip and grill the other side.
