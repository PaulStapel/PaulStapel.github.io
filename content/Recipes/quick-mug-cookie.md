FAST

## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp butter
- 5 tbsp flour
- 2 - 3 tbsp milk
- 2 tbsp sugar
- a pinch of baking soda
- Some optional toppings, like chocolate chips or fruits. 

## Cooking plan
- Melt your butter
- Whisk everything together in a microwaveable mug or small bowl 
- Put in the microwave for 1 minute

## ENJOY!!
